HANIBAL - THE BATTLE OF CANNAE

After defeating the Romans at the battle of the lake. Tranimene, Hanibal now moved his forces down south. Thre was panic in Rome and Quitis Fabius Maximus was appointed dictator to deal with Hanibal. Fabius, however, refuse to meet Hanibal in the open field and this strategy became highly unpopula in Rome.
